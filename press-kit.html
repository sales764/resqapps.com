üìñ Company Description

Short Version (50 words):
RESQ+ is Thailand's leading emergency response platform, providing instant access to ambulance services, emergency alerts, and AI-powered medical guidance. Our mission is to save lives through technology and rapid response coordination.

Long Version (150 words):
RESQ+ is revolutionizing emergency response in Thailand with cutting-edge technology and comprehensive safety solutions. Our platform connects users instantly to ambulance services (1669), provides real-time emergency alerts, and offers AI-powered medical guidance during critical situations.

Founded in 2024, RESQ+ serves over 10,000 active users across Thailand. Our app features include one-tap emergency calling, GPS location sharing, AI Emergency Nurse for medical guidance, multilingual support (English, French, Thai, Chinese), and integration with Thailand's national emergency systems.

We partner with the Thai Red Cross, local hospitals, and government agencies to ensure the fastest possible response times. Our goal is to reduce emergency response times by 50% and save countless lives through technology-driven solutions.
