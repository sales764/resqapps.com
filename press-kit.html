📖 Company Description

Short Version (50 words):
RESQ+ is an Android emergency response app built for real-world preparedness in Thailand. It combines SOS tools, AI Emergency Nurse guidance, Team Connect, Community Alerts, Rush Map, and emergency profile features to help users access support faster during urgent situations.

Long Version (150 words):
RESQ+ is an Android emergency response application designed to support users during urgent situations in Thailand. The app combines one-tap access to emergency services, SOS tools, AI Emergency Nurse guidance, Team Connect coordination, Community Alerts, Rush Map, First Aid guidance, and emergency profile management in one experience.

Founded in 2024, RESQ+ is available on Google Play and currently supports English, French, Thai, Spanish, Italian, and Filipino in the app. It also includes weather, marine, compass, and utility tools that help users prepare before and during emergencies.

RESQ+ is positioned as an emergency support tool and does not replace professional emergency services. Media coverage should describe it as a mobile app that helps users organize critical information, access guidance, and coordinate emergency-related actions from their phones.
