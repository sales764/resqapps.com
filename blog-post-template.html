10 Essential Emergency Safety Tips Everyone Should Know

Dr. Sarah Chen
November 1, 2024 ‚Ä¢ 5 min read

In emergency situations, knowing the right actions to take can mean the difference between life and death. Here are 10 essential safety tips that everyone should know.

1. Know Emergency Numbers

The first step in emergency preparedness is knowing who to call. In Thailand, the emergency number is 1669 for ambulance services and 191 for police.

üí° Pro Tip: Save these numbers in your phone with ICE (In Case of Emergency) prefix.

2. Learn Basic First Aid

Basic first aid knowledge can save lives. Learn CPR, how to stop bleeding, and how to treat burns. Consider taking a certified first aid course.

Essential First Aid Skills:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)
  • Heimlich Maneuver (for choking)
  • Wound care and bandaging
  • Burn treatment
  • Recognizing signs of stroke

3. Create an Emergency Kit

Every household should have an emergency kit ready. This kit should contain essential supplies that can sustain your family for at least 72 hours.

4. Have an Evacuation Plan

Know the fastest routes out of your home, workplace, and neighborhood. Practice evacuation drills with your family.

"In emergencies, people don't rise to the occasion, they fall to their level of training." - Navy SEALs saying

5. Stay Informed

Download emergency alert apps and sign up for local emergency notifications. Stay informed about potential hazards in your area.
