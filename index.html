❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RESQ+

💰

How much does RESQ+ cost?

RESQ+ offers a 7-day free trial. After the trial, you can choose between a monthly subscription or an annual subscription (which gives you 2 months free).
🌐

Does it work offline?

Many features work offline, including First Aid guidance, Emergency Profile, and core SOS tools. AI Nurse, team features, community alerts, weather, and weather data require an internet connection.
🔒

Is my medical data safe?

Emergency profiles are stored locally on your device. When you use connected features such as sign-in, alerts, notifications, team sync, or AI guidance, related operational data may be processed by connected cloud services.
🤖

How accurate is the AI Medical Nurse?

The AI follows Red Cross ABC+D triage protocols and provides evidence-based guidance. However, it's a support tool - always call emergency services (1669) for life-threatening situations.
📱

Which devices are supported?

RESQ+ works on Android 6.0 and above. It's designed for phones and tablets.
🌍

What languages are supported?

The app currently supports English, French, Thai, Spanish, Italian, and Filipino. AI Emergency Nurse guidance is available in those supported languages.
👥

How many people can join a team?

Teams can have unlimited members. Perfect for hiking groups, family trips, school outings, or corporate events.
🔋

Does it drain battery?

RESQ+ is optimized for low battery usage. Team GPS tracking uses minimal power and you can disable features you're not using to save battery.
🚨

What happens when I press SOS?

SOS can trigger flash Morse code, emergency recording, SMS alerts when enabled and permitted, and quick access to emergency services.
📊

What data do you collect?

RESQ+ processes the data needed to deliver connected features such as sign-in, alerts, notifications, and AI responses. Emergency profile details stay on your device unless you choose to share information through supported features.
