Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RESQ+

üí∞

Is RESQ+ really free?

Yes! RESQ+ is 100% free forever. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. We believe emergency response should be accessible to everyone.
üåê

Does it work offline?

Many features work offline including First Aid guidance, Emergency Profile, and SOS activation. AI Nurse and Team GPS require internet connection.
üîí

Is my medical data safe?

Absolutely. We DO NOT collect personal data (names, photos, medical info, insurance). All your emergency profile data stays locally on YOUR device, encrypted. We only collect anonymous usage statistics to improve response times and save more lives. GDPR compliant.
ü§ñ

How accurate is the AI Medical Nurse?

The AI follows Red Cross ABC+D triage protocols and provides evidence-based guidance. However, it's a support tool - always call emergency services (1669) for life-threatening situations.
üì±

Which devices are supported?

RESQ+ works on Android 7.0 and above. It's optimized for both phones and tablets with responsive design for all screen sizes.
üåç

What languages are supported?

RESQ+ supports English, French, Thai, and Chinese with full translation of all features including the AI Medical Nurse conversations.
üë•

How many people can join a team?

Teams can have unlimited members. Perfect for hiking groups, family trips, school outings, or corporate events.
üîã

Does it drain battery?

RESQ+ is optimized for low battery usage. Team GPS tracking uses minimal power and you can disable features you're not using to save battery.
üö®

What happens when I press SOS?

The SOS Smart System activates flash morse code, starts video recording, can send SMS to emergency contacts, and provides quick access to emergency services (1669).
üìä

What data do you collect?

We ONLY collect anonymous usage statistics (feature usage, response times, app performance) to improve RESQ+ and save more lives every second. We do NOT collect personal data, names, photos, medical information, or insurance details. Your privacy is our priority.
